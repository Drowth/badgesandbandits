How to Play

A quick-start guide to understanding the dusty showdowns and sneaky bluffs of Badges & Bandits.

Objective

You're either a law-aligned Badge or a devious Bandit. Badges want to protect the town and hang the Ringleader. Bandits want to pull off 3 Heists — or take out the Sheriff.

Game Setup

  1. Deal each player a secret Role Card
  2. Lay out all 5 Location Cards
  3. Each player gets a hand of 6 Action Cards
  4. Place Suspicion and Damage Tokens nearby
  5. Shuffle Item Cards (Curses, Pistol, Armor, etc.)
  6. Set the Round Tracker and Heist Pile in the center

Role Assignment

Option 1: Standard — Everyone gets a defined role (e.g., Sheriff, Bandit, etc.)

Option 2: Ringleader Recruitment — All roles are blank. Ringleader secretly selects 1–2 allies by tapping hands during a blind phase.

Round Structure

  1. Pick Locations: Players draft which spot to visit
  2. Play Actions: Heist, Buy, Shoot, Stakeout, Lay Low, Defend
  3. Resolve by Location: All cards are revealed and resolved one location at a time
  4. Apply Results: Suspicion, Damage, and Heist Tokens applied
  5. Gallows Vote: After Rounds 3 and 5, the most suspicious players face judgment

Victory Conditions

  • Bandits Win: 3 Heists completed or Sheriff is eliminated
  • Badges Win: Ringleader is hanged after ≥1 Heist, or no Heists occur by Round 5

Snake Oil Curses

  • Grizzly Growl: Forces players to flee the location
  • Rattlesnake Reflex: Instantly shoot back if attacked
  • Cactus Cramp: Prevent another player from Buying
  • Howling Moon Fever: Player must speak with a lisp for the rest of the game
